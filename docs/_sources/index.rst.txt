Welcome this Hands-on-Labs Workshops Repository! 
=====================================================================

We are excited to make available to our customers, partners, and other Microsoft communities a series of ½ day product hands-on-labs workshops they can easily run in their environments. 

These workshops are designed to be interactive, collaborative and help Devs and Ops teams gain real hands-on experience with Microsoft Azure Applications Solutions. In just a few hours, they will have sharpen their technical skills and gain new product knowledge though real case scenarios. 
This repository has been created by the Digital & Application Innovation Cloud Solution Architect team. If you’d like to contribute to this repository with new product HoL content, please contact jonathan.machado@microsoft.com and mdougados@microsoft.com.

   Work in progress... and more to come!
