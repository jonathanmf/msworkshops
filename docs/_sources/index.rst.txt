What is a Hands-on-Lab Workshop?
The Hands on Lab (HoL) are tailored to your customer's immediate need and is an opportunity for devs/ops to learn by doing.  They will take an active part in the activity and will gain skill sets on a specific product at speed. Also a great opportunity to create interest on new products and generate cross-sell opportunities
